Click This, Not That

This video demonstrates a participant going through the first two parts of the study, testing tripwires and creating a log in ritual.

This video shows how tripwires work in action. A policy for this user causes them to be logged out after touching two tripwires. After logging in again and touching a third, another policy is triggered which blocks this device.
